Here are some information for "Mode":
  • "-5":The -5-Y Sequence Mode(It can't expand anything).
  • "-4":The -4-Y Sequence Mode(It can only expand sequences of "1"s, and the limit is ω).
  • "-3":The -3-Y Sequence Mode(k+1=k,1,1,1,..., and the limit is ω^2).
  • "-2":The -2-Y Sequence Mode(k+1=k,k,k,..., and the limit is ω^ω).
  • "-1":The -1-Y Sequence Mode(Also known as Address Notation, the limit is ε_0).
  • "P":The PrSS Mode(Primitive Sequence System, the limit is ε_0).
  • "L":The LPrSS Mode(Long PrSS, the limit is φ(ω,0)).
  • "H":The HPrSS Mode(Hyper PrSS, the limit is BO).
  • "IB":The IBMS Mode(BM3.3, the BMS "without upgrading", the limit is SHO).
  • "B":The BMS Mode(Bashicu Matrix System, the limit is SHO).
  • "0":The 0-Y Sequence Mode(The "linear version" of BMS, the limit is SHO).
  • "1":The Y Sequence Mode(The limit is SYO).
  • "n"(n is a positive integer):The Diagonalized n-Y Sequence Mode.(probably wrong)
  • "w":The Weak Magma ω-Y Sequence Mode(The limit is MHO).
